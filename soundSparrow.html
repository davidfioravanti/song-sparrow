ABOUT

SONG SPARROW
is a dynamically built web app that does the heavy-lifting of searching for music info. It can find information on your favorite artists and others you want to discover!

It also has the capability to let you know if an artist is playing any shows in your area! Pretty handy right?

Our team of Junior Full-Stack Web Developers wanted a better way to gather everything you'd want to know about music. Our app is sleek and inspired by the urban-jungle known as the PHILLY MUSIC SCENE.

We hope that this app helps you connect with the artists you love, and to find new music to enjoy!
